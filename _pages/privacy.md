## Privacy Notice

This website uses **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to help me understand how visitors engage with the content. I use this information solely to track page readership — for example, which pages are visited most often — and for no other purpose. No data is shared with third parties beyond Google's analytics infrastructure.

A page view counter (visitor-badge.laobi.icu) is displayed in the footer. It increments an anonymous total once per browsing session and sets no cookies. No personal data is collected or stored.

### What is collected

GA4 automatically collects the following non-personally-identifiable information when you visit this site:

- Pages you visit and how long you stay
- Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referring website or search engine (if you arrived via a link)

GA4 does **not** collect your name, email address, IP address (it is anonymized by default), or precise location.

### Cookies

GA4 uses first-party cookies (`_ga`, `_ga_*`) to distinguish unique visitors and track session activity. These cookies do not store personal information.

### How to opt out

You can prevent GA4 from collecting your data in several ways:

- **Browser extension**: Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) for your browser.
- **Browser settings**: Block third-party cookies or use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting.
- **Ad blockers**: Most ad-blocking extensions (uBlock Origin, AdBlock Plus, etc.) can block Google Analytics scripts.
